Screen 04 · Adherence framing

Progress, not compliance.

"4 of 7 completed" produces continued engagement. "Missed 3 of 7 days" produces shame and abandonment. The numbers are the same. The framing changes everything.

Design rule: the component API only accepts a completed count and a target. There is no "missed" prop, no compliance percentage. This is baked in deliberately so no surface in the product can drift into compliance framing.
